Fanta

So let’s start this story at the beginning. Fanta traces its origins back to World War II. At the time, the Coca-Cola business in Germany was one of the world’s most successful, second only to the company’s sales in the United States. The modern version of Fanta. The Fanta we know now was born in Naples, Italy, where in 1955 Coca-Cola began using local citrus to formulate the now famous orange flavour. Bright, bubbly and popular, Fanta is the soft drink that intensifies fun. Introduced in 1940, Fanta is the second oldest brand of The Coca-Cola Company.

I designed a 3D model in Blender, a comprehensive 3D modeling software, and exported it in the GLB (GL Transmission Format Binary) format, which is a widely-used format for 3D models optimized for web applications. After exporting, I integrated the model into my website using Three.js, a powerful JavaScript library for creating and displaying 3D graphics in a web browser. By writing a script that initializes a Three.js scene, sets up a camera and lighting, and loads the GLB model with the GLTFLoader, I was able to render the 3D model interactively on the webpage, enhancing the visual experience for users.
